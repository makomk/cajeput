Welcome to the Cajeput pre-release

This software is highly experimental, for testing purposes only, and not release-worthy. Most of the features you'd expect to have are incomplete or not implemented yet.

Currently supported:

  • Teleports into and out of the region, as well as direct login. Minus any attachments.
  • Logging out cleanly (though without saving attachments)
  • Chat, including chat from and to objects
  • Avatar movement (including most of the animations)
  • A small but respectable subset of LSL, with quite a few bugs

Still to do

This isn't Cajeput-specific, though - it looks like OpenSim is known to have issues in this area.

Welcome to the Cajeput pre-release

This software is highly experimental, for testing purposes only, and not release-worthy. Most of the features you'd expect to have are incomplete or not implemented yet.

Currently supported:

  • Teleports into the region, and even out (most of the time), as well as direct logins
  • Logging out cleanly (though without saving attachments, so be careful)
  • Chat, including chat from (though not to) objects
  • Avatar movement (including most of the animations)
  • Some very basic and buggy LSL scripting support

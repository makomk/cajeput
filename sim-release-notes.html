Welcome to the Cajeput pre-release

This software is highly experimental, for testing purposes only, and not release-worthy. Most of the features you'd expect to have - inventory, teleportation and region crossing, even objects - are not implemented yet.

If you wish to leave the region, please logout and log back in somewhere else. (You should be logged out cleanly, though this also means that you'll return here if you just log in at your previous location.)

Currently supported:

  • Teleports into the region (mostly), and even out (occasionally)
  • Logging into the region directly (again, mostly)
  • Logging out cleanly
  • Chat
  • Avatar movement (including most of the animations)
